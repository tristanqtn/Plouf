Our Services

Plouf is a numerical pool log book designed to help you keep track of all your pool-related activities and maintenance tasks. Whether you are a pool owner or a professional pool service provider, Plouf offers a range of features to suit your needs.

Usage:

  • Log Book: Record and monitor pool maintenance activities.
  • Water Quality Tracking: Keep track of water quality parameters.
  • Service Scheduling: Schedule and manage pool service appointments.

Advantages:

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to navigate and use.
  • Customizable: Tailor the platform to fit your specific requirements.
  • Secure: Your data is protected with top-notch security measures.

About the Project

This project is developed and maintained by a single individual. The initial goal was to solve a pool log storage issue, which then evolved into an opportunity to build a full-stack application to keep coding skills sharp. This application is designed to be run at home, allowing everyone to access the data easily.
